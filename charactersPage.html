Meet the Characters

Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is a special grade Jujutsu sorcerer and the teacher of Yuji Itadori. He possesses immense power and is known for his charismatic personality. His laid-back attitude often belies the strength he possesses, making him one of the most powerful sorcerers in the world.

Yuji Itadori

Yuji is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a kind-hearted and brave high school student who becomes a Jujutsu sorcerer after consuming a cursed object. His determination to save others and his innate physical abilities make him a formidable fighter, despite his lack of experience.

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi is a skilled Jujutsu sorcerer and a classmate of Yuji. He uses Shikigami to fight and is known for his serious demeanor. Despite his calm exterior, he is fiercely loyal to his friends and will do anything to protect them, often battling his own internal struggles.

Maki Zenin

Maki is a strong-willed Jujutsu sorcerer from the Zenin family. She strives to prove herself despite the family's prejudice against her. With her extraordinary combat skills and determination, Maki fights against the expectations of her family and seeks to carve her own path as a sorcerer.

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara is a confident and fierce Jujutsu sorcerer who values her friends and has a strong sense of justice. Her unique combat style and charm make her a standout character, and she is not afraid to challenge anyone who threatens her or her friends.

Ryomen Sukuna

Sukuna is a powerful curse and the main antagonist of the series. He was once a human sorcerer and is known as the "King of Curses." His overwhelming strength and cunning intelligence pose a great threat to both sorcerers and curses alike, making him a pivotal figure in the story.
