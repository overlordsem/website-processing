Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular anime and manga series created by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes embroiled in the world of jujutsu sorcery after consuming a cursed object: the finger of the powerful curse known as Sukuna. As Yuji grapples with his newfound powers and responsibilities, he joins the Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical School to learn how to battle curses and protect humanity.

The series features captivating characters such as Megumi Fushiguro, who uses shadow manipulation techniques, and Nobara Kugisaki, a fierce and determined sorceress. Together, they face formidable adversaries, delve into the origins of curses, and explore themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the struggle between good and evil. As the story unfolds, the bonds between these characters grow stronger, making the stakes even higher in their battles against curses and the mysterious organization behind them.
